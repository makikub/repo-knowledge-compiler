# Repo-kb as raw log wiki

## Original or Sanitized Note

Feedback from dogfooding repo-kb in another repository: init must not create or imply project guidance under .claude/rules. Repo-kb is intended as an LLM wiki where raw logs from incidents, discussions, debugging sessions, reviews, and problem solving are stored first. Later, during weekly/monthly or explicit maintenance, an LLM should consult .repo-kb and promote only necessary stable lessons into CLAUDE.md, .claude/rules, REVIEW.md, or docs.
